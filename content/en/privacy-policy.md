### Tracking Technologies and Cookies
So, we totally use Cookies and other neat tracking tech to keep tabs on what's happening on our Service and stash away a bit of info. We're talking beacons, tags, and scripts – all designed to collect and track data, helping us spruce up and understand our Service better. The tech we might be using could include: 🍪

- **Cookies or Browser Cookies**: Think of a Cookie as a tiny file that gets placed on your device. You can totally tell your browser to give all Cookies the cold shoulder or to ping you whenever one's about to be sent. But here's the kicker: if you don't vibe with Cookies, some parts of our Service might just play hard to get. Unless you've already set your browser to reject them, our Service will probably be using Cookies.
- **Web Beacons**: So, some parts of our Service and even our emails might have these tiny electronic files called web beacons (you might also hear them called clear GIFs, pixel tags, or single-pixel GIFs). What do they do? Well, they let the Company, for example, count users who've checked out those pages or opened an email, and they're super handy for other website stats (like seeing how popular a certain section is or double-checking our system and server integrity). Pretty cool, huh? 😎

Alright, so Cookies come in two flavors: 'Persistent' and 'Session.' Think of Persistent Cookies as the ones that stick around on your personal computer or mobile device even after you've logged off. Session Cookies, though? Poof! They vanish the moment you close your web browser. Simple!

We actually use *both* Session and Persistent Cookies, and here's why:

- **Necessary / Essential Cookies**
  - **Type**: Session Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are super essential for giving you the services available on our Website and letting you use some of its cool features. They're like our trusty guardians, helping to verify users and keep those pesky fraudulent account uses at bay. Seriously, without these Cookies, the services you're asking for just wouldn't happen, and we only use them to make sure you get what you need! ✨

- **Cookies Policy / Acceptance Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies simply let us know if you've given us the thumbs-up to use Cookies on our Website. Pretty straightforward!

- **Functionality Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are the ones that help us remember all the awesome choices you make while using our Website – like keeping your login info handy or remembering your favorite language. The whole point here is to give you a super personalized experience, so you don't have to keep typing in your preferences every single time you visit! How convenient is that? 🤩

Wanna know more about the Cookies we use and what choices you've got? No sweat! Just hop over to our Cookies Policy or check out the Cookies section right here in our Privacy Policy. All the deets are there!
