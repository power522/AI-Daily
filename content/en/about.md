#### Featured Work

*   **[Open-Source Contribution/AIClient-2-API](https://github.com/justlovemaki/AIClient-2-API)**:
    *   AIClient-2-API is a powerful API proxy service. It takes client-only large language model (LLM) capabilities (like those from Gemini CLI, Antigravity, Qwen Code, Kiro) and wraps them into a local OpenAI-compatible interface. This service also handles account pool management, intelligent polling, automatic failover, and system prompt management. Pretty cool, right? 😎
*   **[Open-Source Contribution/OpenClaw-Docker-CN-IM](https://github.com/justlovemaki/OpenClaw-Docker-CN-IM)**:
    *   OpenClaw-Docker-CN-IM is a Docker image that integrates Chinese IM plugins. This image comes pre-installed and configured with plugins for major Chinese IM platforms such as Feishu, DingTalk, QQ Bot, and WeChat Work, enabling quick deployment of a multi-platform AI bot gateway. Super handy for your AI bot needs! 🚀
